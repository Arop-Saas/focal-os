FAQ

Common questions

How does the 14-day free trial work?
Sign up with no credit card required. You get full access to all features on the Pro plan for 14 days. After the trial, you choose a plan that fits — or your account goes read-only. No surprise charges.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Does Focal OS take a cut of my payments?
No. Focal OS charges a flat monthly fee — we never take a percentage of your revenue. Stripe's standard processing fees (typically 2.9% + 30¢) apply to card payments, but that goes to Stripe, not us.
How do brokerage pricing groups work?
Create a group (e.g. "RE/MAX VIP — 15% off"), assign clients to it, and every invoice generated for those clients automatically includes the discount as a named line item. It shows on the invoice so your clients see the saving.
Can multiple photographers use the same account?
Absolutely. Invite your whole team — photographers, editors, virtual assistants — each with their own login and role-based permissions. Everyone sees what they need, nothing they don't.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication is handled by Supabase with support for magic-link logins. We never share or sell your data.
